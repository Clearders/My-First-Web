Two months. Seven countries. Countless bus rides, night trains, and ferry crossings.
Backpacking through Southeast Asia was the most transformative experience of my life — and
it cost less than I spend in a typical month at home.

## The Route

I flew into Bangkok, then slowly made my way through Thailand, Laos, Vietnam, Cambodia,
Malaysia, Indonesia, and Singapore. No rigid itinerary — just a rough direction and an
open mind.

## The Highlights

- Watching the sunrise over Angkor Wat in Cambodia
- Motorcycling through the misty hills of Pai, Thailand
- Eating bánh mì on a street corner in Hội An, Vietnam — for 50 cents
- Snorkelling the crystal waters of the Gili Islands, Indonesia

## What I Learned

Slow down. You'll always wish you'd spent more time somewhere and less time rushing
to the next destination. Talk to locals. Learn three words of the local language — it
changes everything.

> "Travel is the only thing you buy that makes you richer."

If you're on the fence about doing a trip like this — just go. Start planning today.
Your future self will thank you. 🌏
