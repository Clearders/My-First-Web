Tokyo is a city of extremes — towering skyscrapers beside ancient shrines, buzzing
izakayas steps from silent gardens. Its café culture is no different: wonderfully
diverse and consistently excellent.

## Koffee Mameya, Omotesando

A reservation-only coffee experience where knowledgeable staff guide you through a
carefully curated selection of single-origin beans. Minimalist, meditative, extraordinary.

## Fuglen Tokyo, Tomigaya

Transplanted from Oslo, Fuglen brings Scandinavian design and specialty coffee to a
quiet Tokyo neighbourhood. The vintage furniture and natural light make it perfect for
an afternoon with a book.

## Cat Cafés

No trip to Tokyo is complete without a cat café. Neko no Niwa in Jimbocho is one of
the best: relaxed atmosphere, resident cats of all breeds, and decent coffee to boot.

Tokyo has a café made for you. ☕
